Inverse Problems Symposium Presentation

 Mon 04 June 2018 by Dr. Dirk Colbry

I was invited to give a talk at the inverse seminars symposium. I was a little nervous since I don't consider myself an expert in inverse problems. However after talking to the organizers (Kirk and James) what they wanted was a high level talk and overview of advanced computing. This was something I could do! I put together snippets from a variety of my previous talks.

Originally I planned for a 60 minute talk but only ended up with an estimated 45 minutes (at most) of content. Fortunately, the organizers actually wanted a shorter talk and in the end I think everything turned out to be around 30 minutes with questions.

Overall the Inverse Problems Symposium was a great event and I learned a lot. I was honored to be invited to give a talk and learned a lot. I was even considering attending next year.

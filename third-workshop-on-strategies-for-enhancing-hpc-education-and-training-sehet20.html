Third Workshop on Strategies for Enhancing HPC Education and Training (SEHET20)

Mon 27 July 2020 by Dr. Dirk Colbry

I presented an overview of my CMSE401 course (methods in parallel programming) at the SEHET20 Workshop Program put on by the SigHPC Education Chapter.

The presentation ("The Design of a Practical Flipped Classroom Model for Teaching Parallel Programming to Undergraduates") focused mostly on how I designed the course using a flipped classroom model and jupyter notebooks.
